This is the official lore for the Altum tabletop ropleplaying game!

The Flood!

Stories have it that 25,000 years ago The Leviathan made the sky cry and it weeped for a millennia causing the planet to flood. This godly being also brought an army of unworldy creatures. After that nothing has been the same. We now really only refer to time as BF (Before Flood) or AF (After Flood). Currency after the flood is only two main components, bullets and drinkable water. People have started to make boat homes and connect them with other boat homes essentially making a floating city. There are still bits of lands left. We got islands popping up and disappearing on the daily. Small civilizations have been populating these bits of lands, but that's usually only the wealthy.

World landscape (or whats left...)

The world is mostly water with islands scattered around. Some trees have adapted to grow in and break the oceans surface.
Some civilizations (mostly made of Ape folk) live in these giant trees.

Setting

This is a post apocalyptic world that is based off of the pirate era, steampunk and a mix of modern day world. It also has some cosmic horror/lovecraftian influence.
There also a fantasy influence so theres magic.

Currency

100 bullets = 1 gallon of water
For reference a row boat cost about 50 gallons of water.

The Leviathan

The Leviathan is a godly being that has flooded and plauged this world with its spawn.
The main Leviathan is a legendary creature that is extremely rare to encouter and will wipe out almost any party. How ever its offspring is a different story.
Many brave adventures have taken on the likes of these monsters and lived to tell the tale.
